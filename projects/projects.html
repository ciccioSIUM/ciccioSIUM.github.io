Akinator-STEAM

An Akinator-style educational game that challenges the user to think of a figure from the STEAM world. Through 18 binary questions, the algorithm progressively eliminates the 25 characters in the database until it guesses who you have in mind, all accompanied by a fact sheet about the revealed character.
